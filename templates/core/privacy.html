Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "F j, Y" %}

What we collect

  • Account info — username, email, optional full name, optional profile photo.
  • Learning data — enrollments, lesson progress, mock-exam attempts & scores, notes you write.
  • Payment metadata — plan chosen, gateway used, transaction reference. We never store your card numbers; those stay with Stripe / PayPal / eSewa / Khalti.
  • Support chats — messages you send to our bot or human agents, retained to improve answers.

How we use it

To operate the learning platform, track your progress, deliver paid content, and improve the product. We don't sell your data to advertisers. We don't run third-party ad trackers.

Kids' accounts (under 13)

Kids' accounts are parent-managed. We collect the minimum needed to deliver lessons and track progress. Parents can request export or deletion of a child's data anytime — contact us.

Cookies

We use session cookies for login and a CSRF cookie for security. No advertising cookies.

Data export & deletion

You can request a full export of your data, or deletion of your account, via the contact form. We honour requests within 30 days.

Security

Passwords are hashed with Django's PBKDF2. Media files served through gated views when tied to paid content. We aim for industry-standard protection but can't guarantee 100% against every threat.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email hello@pathlab.np or open the support chat.
